## English

Business Strategy is an MBA-style Codex skill for business strategy, financial analysis, market analysis, valuation, fundraising preparation, and strategic reviews. It emphasizes commercial judgment, financial discipline, and actionable recommendations.

### Best For

- Business plans and startup strategy
- P&L, cash flow, unit economics, and financial health checks
- Market sizing, competitive analysis, and business model review
- Valuation, fundraising narrative, and pitch deck preparation
- SWOT, Porter's Five Forces, growth strategy, and operating diagnosis

### What It Does

- Breaks business questions down with strategic and financial frameworks
- Examines revenue quality, cost structure, cash conversion, and growth sustainability
- Surfaces risks, evidence, and practical recommendations
- Supports workflows for business planning, financial analysis, market analysis, valuation, and strategic review
- Works well alongside spreadsheets, documents, and research materials

### Usage

Install or copy this repository into your Codex skills directory, then ask Codex:

```text
Use $Businessstrategy analyze this business plan, financial model, or market opportunity: ...
```
